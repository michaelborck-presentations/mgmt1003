**AI Boardroom: Leveraging AI for Business Strategy**
*One-Hour Interactive Online Seminar*

---

**Session Overview**

Learn how to use AI as a strategic thinking partner for business decision-making. This interactive session will teach you practical techniques for generating better insights, challenging your assumptions, and making more informed business decisions using AI.

---

**Session Outline**

**1. The AI Mindset (10 minutes)**
- Understanding AI as a "reasoning engine" not just a search tool
- The "Intern" analogy: working with AI as a collaborative partner
- The golden rule: AI should challenge your thinking, not replace it

**2. The "Board of Directors" Strategy (15 minutes)**
- Multi-perspective prompting for business decisions
- Live demonstration: analysing a business idea through multiple stakeholder lenses
- How to use AI to simulate different organisational roles (CFO, Marketing Director, Operations Manager)

**3. The Devil's Advocate Technique (10 minutes)**
- Using AI to identify weaknesses in your strategy
- How to reduce confirmation bias in business planning
- Building resilience through critical analysis

**4. Reverse Prompting: Let AI Interview You (10 minutes)**
- When you don't know what questions to ask
- Having AI clarify your business problems through targeted questions
- Generating tailored strategies based on your specific context

**5. Ethics & Responsible Use (10 minutes)**
- Data privacy: what not to share with AI tools
- Recognising and managing AI bias
- Fact-checking and verification strategies

**6. Q&A and Resources (5 minutes)**

---

**Key Takeaways**
- Practical prompting frameworks you can use immediately
- Strategies for better business analysis and decision-making
- Resources for continued learning
